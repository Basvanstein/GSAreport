Morris analysis results

The elementary effects calculated by the Morris [1] algorithm. On the left the covariance plot with mu_star, the absolute of the mean elementary effect, against sigma, the standard deviation of the elementary effect. Points highlighted are the parameters with the highest mu_star and these are also shown on the right with the confidence of the algorithm.

RBD Fast and Pawn

On the left the S1 results of RBD-FAST - Random Balance Designs Fourier Amplitude Sensitivity Test [4]. On the right the minimum, median and maximum results from the PAWN method [5]. The PAWN method is a moment-independent approach to GSA. It is described as producing robust results at relatively low sample sizes.
